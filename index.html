Everything you need to Know

The Master of Business Administration (M.B.A.) program is designed to equip you with essential modern management knowledge, skills and tools to ensure your mindset, skill set and application ability for the business growth and sustainability. This MBA programme is accredited by our academic partner universities which are one of the best in class in the global education. Our MBA Graduates are highly confident on the business management along with the subject matter socialization to contribute to the organization for its growth.

Corporates also appoint our MBA graduate with hoe and confidence for their professional skill set and business centric mentality. In this course you will study and learn modern tools and techniques on the particular subject along with business areas. The curriculum has been developed by a pool of industry expert on the particular area with their expert contribution in the respective field.

Entry Requirements and Eligibility

Entry Requirements:

Recognized Bachelor Degree in any field or Recognized Post Graduate Diploma (PGD).

Sufficient English language proficiency.

At least 1 year of working experience.

Participants must have the facility of smart phone or laptop or computer to maintain the program activity.

Mature Entry Route (done on case-by-case evaluation basis only):

  • Candidates without any prior tertiary education and qualification should possess at least 10 years of professional working experience in the field of Business, Management, or related areas of work.
  • Candidates who have completed HND Level 4 (Diploma) should possess at least 7 years of professional working experience in the field of Business, Management, or related areas of work.
  • Candidates who have completed HND Level 5 (Higher Diploma) should possess at least 4 years of professional working experience in the field of Business, Management, or related areas of working experience.
